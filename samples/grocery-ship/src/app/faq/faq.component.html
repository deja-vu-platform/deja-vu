FAQ

For requesters:

Q: What is the use of the minimum shopper rating in the request form?
A: Each request can only be seen and claimed by users with shipping ratings at least equal to the set minimum shopper rating. You can set a minimum shopper rating to ensure the shipping service quality.

Q: What happens if the shopper does not show up on time?
A: You can contact the shopper again or reject the delivery with the reason that “The deliverer did not show up.”. You should rate the shopper accordingly. Shoppers with shipping rating below our set threshold will be suspended from delivering for a period of time.

Q: What if the shopper buys a wrong item?
A: You can reject the delivery and give the reason that “The delivered item is not what I requested for.” The shopper will be responsible for either returning and keeping the item. You should rate the shopper accordingly. Shoppers with shipping rating below our set threshold will be suspended from delivering for a period of time.

Q: How do I make sure that the item price entered by the shopper is correct?
A: We recommend asking the shopper to show the receipt to you when you pick up the items. If the price is wrong, you may make your own arrangements with the shopper to sort out the price difference. You can then Accept or Reject the delivery with the appropriate rating for the shopper. Note that if you accept the delivery, you will pay the incorrect price entered by the shopper. If you reject the delivery, you may select the rejection reason, "The price of the delivered item is incorrect."

For shoppers:

Q: When should I claim a request?
A: You claim a request when you are at the grocery store(s) and know that you can buy the item in the request.

Q: How do I make sure that a requester pays?
A: We recommend that you ask the requester to pay by clicking Accept on the delivery notification when you deliver the items.

Q: What do I do if I can't contact the requester after buying the item?
A: You can close the delivery on your dashboard and rate the requester accordingly. You can choose to keep or return the item. All the grocery stores in our listing have full refund policies.

Q: What happens if the requester rejects my delivery?
A: If you are unsatisfied with the rejection reason, you rate the requester accordingly. You can choose to keep or return the item. All the grocery stores in our listing have full refund policies.

Q: What is the suspension policy in GroceryShip?
A: If a user deliveries four or more times, and gets an average shipping rating lower than three, the user will be suspended for a month.
